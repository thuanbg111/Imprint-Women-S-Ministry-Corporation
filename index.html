IMPRINT WOMEN'S MINISTRY CORPORATION

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our purpose is to offer resources, training, activities, mentoring, and support to women of all ages at all stages so they can thrive in family, community, and career.

  • Mentoring & peer‑support circles

Workshops & Training

Mentoring & Support

Healthy activities and community events that foster connection.
