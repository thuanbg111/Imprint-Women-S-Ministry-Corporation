About Imprint Women's Ministry

IMPRINT WOMEN'S MINISTRY CORPORATION is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law for charitable purposes. We operate as a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit with EIN 86-2199102.

Specific purpose: We offer resources, training, activities, mentoring, and support to women of all ages at all stages.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
